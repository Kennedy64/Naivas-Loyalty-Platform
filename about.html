About Naivas Loyalty & Mobile Shopping App

Enhancing customer shopping experiences through technology and rewards.

Who We Are

Naivas Supermarket is one of Kenya's leading retail chains, serving millions of customers across the country. The Naivas Loyalty & Mobile Shopping App is designed to make shopping more convenient while rewarding customers for their loyalty.

Through the application, customers can browse products, track loyalty points, receive personalized offers, and enjoy a seamless shopping experience from their mobile devices.

Mission

To provide customers with a convenient, rewarding, and innovative shopping experience through digital technology.

Vision

To become Kenya's most customer-focused retail platform by combining exceptional service with digital innovation.

Core Values

Integrity, Customer Satisfaction, Innovation, Teamwork, and Continuous Improvement.

Why Choose Naivas?

  • Wide variety of quality products.
  • Convenient mobile shopping experience.
  • Reward points for every purchase.
  • Exclusive promotions and member discounts.
  • Secure and user-friendly platform.
